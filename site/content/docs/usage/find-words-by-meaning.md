### The `--means-like` flag

To search for words that have a similar meaning to a specific word, use the `--means-like` flag.

#### Example

Search for words with a meaning similar to "joy".

```bash
polyhymnia --means-like "joy"
```

```text
gladden
   Score: 40000673
   Part of Speech: syn
   Part of Speech: n
   Part of Speech: prop
   Part of Speech: results_type:primary_rel

delight
   Score: 40000471
   Part of Speech: syn
   Part of Speech: n

rejoice
   Score: 40000465
   Part of Speech: syn
   Part of Speech: v

joyfulness
   Score: 40000355
   Part of Speech: syn
   Part of Speech: n
```
